FAQ

How many team members can I invite?

You can invite as many people as you want. We will be happy for each one to join us.

What is the maximum file upload size?

No more than 2GB. All files in your account must fit your alloted storage space.

How do I reset my password?

Go to your profile and click on change password. An email to your linked account will be sent along with the instructions to reset the account.

Can I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription any time by going to your profile and clicking 'view my subscription' link.

Do you provide additional support?

You can contact our customer service team any time for any additional support required.
